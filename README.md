By understanding and engineering the $K$-operator, it becomes possible to directly manipulate the harmonic states of a magneto-viscous (RMHD) plasma, allowing for the design of reactionless propulsion systems and energy-generation based on the controlled manipulation of the $MC=H^2$ mass-energy conversion.
